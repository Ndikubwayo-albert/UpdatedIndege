About Idg.

Indege Daily Laborers System "IDG" is a web infrastucture developed to facilitate daily workers (casual workers) available in Kigali city especially those spend much time standing on roads waiting for employers to come and pick them for a work.

Idg system is headed by the cell administrations then Idg system is under the control and management of cell in which indege point is located.
We advice any user of this system to contact his nearest cell location in the case that an issue related with interacting with system is met.

Our Missions

Idg has the mandate of planning and implementing the linkage of workers with the employers through a digitalized manner.

Responsibilities

Idg has the following responsibilities:

(a) To implement strategies which expand the access and affordability of of work for daily labors by the means of using Information and Communication Technologies infrastructures and services;

b) To accelerate community development through mainstreaming Information and Communication Technologies in national socio-economic sectors;

c) To coordinate national Information and Communication Technology procurement for commonly procured Information and Communication Technology goods and services;
